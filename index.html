Our Services

Abortion Services

Our facilitiy is equipped to provide all of the preliminary testing and counseling services. Prior to your procedure you will have the ability to meet with a counselor to discuss your decision and options. Additionally, she will explain the procedure as well as your follow up treatment. Along with our counseling services all patients will also have a sonogram performed and provide small samples for blood and urine testing. Our fees are all inclusive. There will be no additional fees for preliminary testing or counseling services.

  • Surgical Abortion Services
  • Non-Surgical Abortion Services
  • The GentleTouch® Manual Vacuum Aspiration Procedure
  • Private Abortion Care

Gynecology Services

We offer full service gynecologic care performed by both male and female Board certified or Eligible OB/GYN’S. We would be happy to assist you with scheduling a convenient appointment. Our gynecologic services include but are not limited to:

  • Annual examinations and pap smears
  • STD testing
  • Vaccinations
  • Birth Control and Family Planning Services
  • Colposcopy
  • Endometrial Ablative Procedures (Novasure)
  • Permanent Birth Control Procedures (Essure)
  • Hysteroscopy
  • Treatments for Cervical Dysplasia (Cone Biopsy and Cervical Cryotherapy)
  • Endometrial Biopsy

Contraceptive Services

Bethany Gynecology offers a wide variety of family planning and contraceptive services. Our OB/Gyn physician’s will discuss your needs and lifestyle and together with you determine the best option. Birth control options include:

  • Oral Contraceptives (the pill)
  • Depo Provera
  • IUD insertions and removals
  • Nuva Ring
